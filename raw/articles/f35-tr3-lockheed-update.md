# Lockheed Martin F-35 Technology Refresh 3 (TR-3) Update

## Key Announcement
Lockheed Martin began deliveries of the first **Technology Refresh 3 (TR-3) configured F-35 aircraft** on **July 19, 2024**.

## Production & Delivery Details
*   **Annual Production Rate:** Maintains an expectation of **156 aircraft per year**.
*   **2024 Delivery Target:** Plans to deliver **between 75 and 110 aircraft** in the second half of 2024.
*   **Configuration:** Deliveries will be **primarily in the TR-3 configuration**.

## Program Status & Negotiations
*   Lockheed Martin is working with the **U.S. Government’s F-35 Joint Program Office** on terms and conditions.
*   Discussions focus on the **timing of final payments** for TR-3 aircraft and a **phased approach to delivery**.
*   The company continues to focus on advancing **TR-3 and Block 4 capabilities** to meet customer mission requirements.

## Executive Statement
> “TR-3 and Block 4 represent a critical evolution in capability and their full development remains a top priority for us. These and further software updates over the life of the program will ensure the F-35 continues to be an effective deterrent and the cornerstone of joint all-domain operations now and decades into the future.”
> 
> **— Bridget Lauderdale, Vice President and General Manager of the F-35 Program, Lockheed Martin**
