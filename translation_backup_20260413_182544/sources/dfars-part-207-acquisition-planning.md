## Text Content
Part 207.106 requires the "use of modular, open architectures to enable competition for upgrades."

This DFARS clause implements statutory MOSA requirements by mandating modular, open architectures specifically to enable competition during technology refresh and upgrade cycles.

## Key Provisions
- **Mandate**: Use of modular, open architectures
- **Purpose**: Enable competition for upgrades
- **Context**: Part of Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS)
- **Relationship**: Implements 10 U.S.C. § 4401 requirements
