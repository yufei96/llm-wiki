## Key Provision

**DFARS 207.106**: Requires the "use of modular, open architectures to enable competition for upgrades."

This is the DFARS implementation of the MOSA statutory requirement (10 USC 4401-4403). It mandates that acquisition planning consider modular open architectures as a requirement, not an option.

## Relevance

DFARS Part 207 is where MOSA enters the acquisition planning process. Before a program even writes an RFP, the planning phase must address open architecture requirements and how they will enable competition for future upgrades.
