# Section 804 - Implementation of Modular Open Systems Approaches and Rights in Interface Software

## (1) Requirement for Modular Open System Approach

Section 2446a of title 10, USC is amended—

(A) in subsection (a), adding: 'Other defense acquisition programs shall also be designed and developed, to the maximum extent practicable, with a modular open system approach to enable incremental development and enhance competition, innovation, and interoperability.'

(B) Key definitions added:
- 'modular system interface': a shared boundary between major systems, major system components, or modular systems, defined by various physical, logical, and functional characteristics
- 'modular system': a weapon system or weapon system component that can execute independently, communicate across component boundaries, and be separated/reconnected

## (2) Rights in Technical Data

- Government Purpose Rights (GPR) in technical data for modular system interfaces
- Applies to interfaces developed at private expense or mixed funding
- Interface must be identified in contract solicitation and contract
