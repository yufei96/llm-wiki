# U.S. Army Acquisition Program Portfolio 2024

## 概述

陆军采办、后勤与技术助理部长（ASA(ALT)）发布的 2024 年陆军采办项目组合概览。涵盖陆军所有武器系统项目，按功能域分类。

## 防空反导（Air and Missile Defense）项目列表

| 项目 | 全称 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| AMDPCS | Air and Missile Defense Planning and Control System | 防空反导规划控制系统 |
| AIAMD | Army Integrated Air and Missile Defense | 陆军综合防空反导 |
| **IBCS** | Integrated Battle Command System | 一体化防空反导作战指挥系统 |
| DE M-SHORAD | Directed Energy Maneuver-Short Range Air Defense | 定向能机动近程防空 |
| FAAD C2 | Forward Area Air Defense Command and Control | 前沿区域防空指挥控制 |
| IFPC-HEL | Indirect Fire Protection Capability – High Energy Laser | 间接火力防护-高能激光 |
| IFPC-HPM | Indirect Fire Protection Capability – High Power Microwave | 间接火力防护-高功率微波 |
| IFPC Inc 2 | Indirect Fire Protection Capability Increment 2 | 间接火力防护能力增量 2 |
| IDDS-A | Iron Dome Defense System – Army | 铁穹防御系统-陆军型 |
| **LTAMDS** | Lower Tier Air and Missile Defense Sensor | 低层防空反导传感器 |
| M-SHORAD Inc 3 | Maneuver-Short Range Air Defense Increment 3 | 机动近程防空增量 3 |
| PAC-3 | Patriot Advanced Capability-3 | 爱国者先进能力-3 |
| **Sentinel A3/A4** | Sentinel Aerial Surveillance Radar | Sentinel 对空监视雷达 |
