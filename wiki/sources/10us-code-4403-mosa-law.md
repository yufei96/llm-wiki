# 10us-code-4403-mosa-law

## Source
[[sources/10uscode4403-require.pdf]]

## Text Content
This section deals with modular system interface requirements:

"Requirements relating to availability of modular system interfaces and support for MOSA."

(a) In General.—To the maximum extent practicable, the Secretary of Defense shall ensure that each major defense acquisition program is designed and developed such that—
(1) the modular system interfaces of the program comply with publicly disclosed or widely known standards;
(2) the modular system interfaces of the program are provided with machine-readable documentation; and
(3) the modular system interfaces of the program are sufficient to enable competition, enable interoperability, and support technology refresh.

(b) Availability.—To the maximum extent practicable, the Secretary of Defense shall ensure that each major defense acquisition program that is planned to receive Milestone A or Milestone B approval after December 31, 2018, is designed and developed to be available with modular system interfaces.

(c) Limitation on Availability of Proprietary Interfaces.—Nothing in this section shall be construed to limit the authority of the Secretary of Defense to—
(1) require a major defense acquisition program to be designed and developed using a modular open systems approach; or
(2) prohibit a major defense acquisition program from being designed and developed using a modular open systems approach.

## Key Provisions
- **Interface Standards**: Must comply with publicly disclosed or widely known standards
- **Documentation**: Must provide machine-readable documentation for interfaces
- **Availability Requirement**: Program must be designed to be available with MOSA interfaces
- **Prohibition Clarification**: Does not limit or prohibit MOSA use
