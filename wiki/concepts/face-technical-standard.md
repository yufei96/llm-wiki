# FACE™ — Future Airborne Capability Environment

Managed by **The Open Group**. A technical standard for avionics systems enabling portability of software components across different platforms and vendors.

## Role in MOSA
FACE is cited as a **compelling success case** for MOSA in the avionics domain. Shah's thesis includes it as a primary case study (Chapter 4, Figures 11-12).

## Key Characteristics
- Published interface specifications
- Vendor-neutral component portability
- Reduces integration cost for avionics software
